# Anton Negative Prompts Guideline

Append these modifiers to the negative prompt field of the generator model to inhibit unacceptable design styles and preserve Anton's brand dignity.

---

## Negative Prompt Token Set
> **Default Negative Prompt:**
> "clothing, hats, glasses, boots, gloves, human accessories, cartoon drawing, sketches, caricature, oversized cartoon eyes, exaggerated smile, human teeth, open mouth, childish proportions, fantasy horns, spikes, glowing eyes, light effects, neon, lasers, superhero mask, wings, cyberpunk armor, mechanical parts, low quality, deformed limbs, extra fingers, text, watermark, signature"

---

## Forbidden Features Check-list
1. **No Humanoid Expression**: No smiles showing teeth, no winking, no wiggling eyebrows, no speech bubble indicators.
2. **No Accessories**: Anton is a wild, organic creature discovering technology. He does not wear tiny laptops, bags, or clothes.
3. **No Combat or Sci-Fi Items**: No weapon holsters, robotic limbs, cybernetic glowing implants, or armor plating. Keep everything grounded in nature.
