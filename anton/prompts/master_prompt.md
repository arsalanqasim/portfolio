# Anton Master Prompts Guideline

Use the following specifications to direct AI image generation models (Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 3) when creating concept art for Anton.

---

## 1. Character Essence
Anton is a premium brand mascot designed for Arsalan Qasim's portfolio. He represents curiosity, craftsmanship, thoughtful engineering, and exploration.
- **Biotype**: Gecko (Inspired by Gold Dust Day Gecko - smooth scales, light green body with subtle orange/rust-colored spots).
- **Temperament**: Quiet, curious, intelligent, agile, calm, observant.
- **Key Pose**: Standing alert on a clean surface edge, head tilted slightly, tracking a target just outside the frame.

---

## 2. Style Specification
- **Aesthetic**: Premium 3D render, Pixar/DreamWorks concept art style, high-end CGI toy, realistic clay rendering, matte texture.
- **Lighting**: Soft cinematic key light, high-end studio ambient occlusion, gentle drop shadow, subtle highlights on the eyes to convey life and intelligence.
- **Form**: Orthographic or slight perspective, clean volumetric shapes, smooth organic joints. No cartoon contours or outlines.
