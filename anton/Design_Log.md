# Design Decision Log

This log records all locked design decisions for Anton the Gecko. Every decision must align with the Core Philosophy and is stored here as a permanent reference.

---

## Decision #01
- **Category**: Speech & Voice
- **Status**: LOCKED 🔒
- **Date**: 2026-06-30
- **Description**: Anton never talks. No speech bubbles, dialog, or vocal sounds.
- **Reason**: Maintains the aura of a quiet, curious observer, avoiding corporate chatbot tropes and distraction.

---

## Decision #02
- **Category**: Attention Seekers
- **Status**: LOCKED 🔒
- **Date**: 2026-06-30
- **Description**: Anton never waves on arrival, jumps, dances, or taps the screen to demand visitor attention.
- **Reason**: Visitors should discover Anton organically. Attention must be earned through curiosity, not requested by the mascot.

---

## Decision #03
- **Category**: Physical Traversal
- **Status**: LOCKED 🔒
- **Date**: 2026-06-30
- **Description**: Anton's locomotion relies strictly on rapid, scurrying gecko movements followed by abrupt freezes.
- **Reason**: Emulates real reptilian physics, making movement feel organic, weighted, and intentional.

---

## Decision #04
- **Category**: Camera Perspective
- **Status**: LOCKED 🔒
- **Date**: 2026-06-30
- **Description**: The primary character view is a flat orthographic 2D plane snapped to the portfolio container boundaries.
- **Reason**: Prevents rendering complexity, ensures fast load times, and simplifies interaction coordinates across viewports.
